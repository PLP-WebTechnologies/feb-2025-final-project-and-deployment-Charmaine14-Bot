Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about working with us

What's the typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A standard website might take 4-8 weeks, while an interior design project could range from 8-16 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes! We regularly work with clients across the country and internationally. For interior design projects, we conduct virtual consultations and site visits as needed.

What's your pricing structure?

We offer both project-based pricing and hourly rates, depending on the nature of the work. After discussing your needs, we'll provide a transparent quote with no hidden fees.

Can you handle both web and interior design for one project?

Absolutely! This is our specialty. We can ensure your digital and physical spaces work harmoniously to create a cohesive brand experience.

What industries do you specialize in?

We've worked with clients across various sectors including tech startups, retail, hospitality, professional services, and creative agencies. Our approach is adaptable to different industries.
